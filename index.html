What RightFitTalent Is

RightFitTalent is a selective engagement initiative.

We work at the intersection of employers and talent where fit truly matters.

If alignment is missing, we prefer not to proceed.

What We Do

  • Engage selectively with employers and job seekers
  • Facilitate discussions where role-fit is critical
  • Operate with milestone-based clarity
  • Use email as the primary mode of communication

What We Do Not Do

  • We are not a job portal
  • We do not collect resumes
  • We do not guarantee hiring
